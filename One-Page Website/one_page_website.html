Food categories

Fruits and Vegetables

You should eat at least five portions of fruit and vegetables a day. They contain important vitamins and minerals that help prevent disease as well as fibre which can lower cholesterol.

Starchy Foods

Starchy foods like potatoes, bread, rice and pasta should make up around a third of what you eat. They're a good source of energy and essential fibre, calcium, iron and vitamins.

Dairy Products

Dairy and dairy alternatives are a good source of protein and vitamins. They also contain calcium, which helps keep our bones healthy and strong.

Meat, fish and alternatives

Meat, fish and eggs are a great source of protein, vitamins and minerals. They are one of the main sources of vitamin B12, an important vitamin which is only found in food from animals like meat and milk.

Fats

Some fat in our diet is essential but most of us eat it too much. Plant-based oils like olive and flaxseed oil are rich in unsaturated fat, so they can help lower cholesterol.
